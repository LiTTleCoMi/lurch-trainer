Instructions

  • Select a strafe from the dropdown on the sidebar then press 'Start' to begin training.

  • While training mode is active; The keys on the keyboard overlay will be tinted 1 of 3 colors indicating what do to next.

    • Green - should be pressed next.

    • Red - should be released next.

    • Blue - should already be held from the previous step.

  • Once you are holding the correct keys it will advance to the next step of the lurch.
